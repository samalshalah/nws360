1) Content Volume & Activity Reports
You can show how much information the world is producing in your system.
• Number of articles per hour / day / week.
• Most active sources (TV channels, websites, influencers, social platforms).
• Which countries or regions are generating the most coverage.
• Peaks in publishing — moments when news suddenly explodes.

This isn’t just statistics; it becomes a heartbeat monitor of the news ecosystem.

2) Trending Topics & Narratives
Here the system stops counting and starts understanding.
• Top trending topics in real time.
• Emerging stories before they dominate headlines.
• Comparison of trends across different countries or languages.
• How a topic rises, peaks, and fades over time.

This is where NWS360 moves from “news collector” to “trend interpreter.”

3) Keyword & Phrase Analysis
Instead of reading thousands of articles, your client sees patterns.
• Most repeated words and phrases.
• Shifts in language around a topic (for example, from “crisis” to “recovery”).
• Client-specific keywords — how often they appear and in what context.
• Heat maps of keyword intensity across time and geography.

Think of this as X-ray vision for language.

4) Sentiment & Tone Reports
News isn’t just facts; it carries emotion and framing.
• Overall sentiment (positive, neutral, negative) by topic or source.
• How different media outlets frame the same story.
• Changes in sentiment over time — for a company, country, or issue.
• Comparison of sentiment across platforms (TV vs social media, for example).

This is where bias and narrative become visible.

5) Source Credibility & Behavior Dashboard
Not in a moral sense, but in a behavioral one.
• Which sources publish fastest.
• Which sources tend to repeat or copy others.
• Which outlets introduce new narratives versus amplify existing ones.
• Source diversity — are clients seeing a broad spectrum or a narrow echo chamber?

6) Client-Custom Reports
This is where NWS360 becomes a business tool.
• Daily / weekly executive summaries automatically generated.
• Alerts when specific keywords spike unexpectedly.
• Custom dashboards per client (government, media, corporate, political).
• Exportable reports (PDF, Excel, or visual slides).

7) Network & Influence Mapping (Advanced Option)
If you want to get truly futuristic:
• How stories travel between platforms.
• Which influencers trigger major news waves.
• Relationships between media outlets based on shared narratives.
• Visual maps showing how information spreads like a virus — but for ideas.
