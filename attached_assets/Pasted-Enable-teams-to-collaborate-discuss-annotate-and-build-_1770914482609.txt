Enable teams to collaborate, discuss, annotate, and build shared understanding directly inside NWS360. The goal is collective intelligence rather than individual consumption.

1) Story Discussions (Internal Conversation Layer)

Each story cluster and article should support discussion threads.

Users can:

comment on an article

discuss a story

reply to others

mention teammates

Store:

comments(
  id,
  user_id,
  target_type (article | story | entity),
  target_id,
  message,
  parent_comment_id,
  timestamp
)


Display comments in a side panel.

2) Annotations & Analyst Notes

Allow users to attach structured notes to any item:

article notes

story notes

entity notes

Types:

observation

warning

hypothesis

conclusion

Store:

annotations(
  id,
  user_id,
  target_type,
  target_id,
  note_type,
  content,
  timestamp
)

3) Shared Briefings (Collaborative Reports)

Users can build custom reports from inside the platform:

Add:

selected stories

charts

written commentary

Export as:

PDF

shareable link

Store:

shared_reports(
  id,
  client_id,
  title,
  created_by,
  last_updated
)

4) Team Workspaces

Allow organizations to create internal groups:

Examples:

Political Monitoring Team

Corporate Risk Team

Regional Desk

Each workspace can have:

its own pinned topics

its own dashboards

its own alerts

Store:

workspaces(id, client_id, name)
workspace_members(workspace_id, user_id)

5) Tagging & Internal Classification

Users can create internal tags:

Examples:

misinformation

competitor activity

reputational risk

Tags are separate from AI topics.

Store:

custom_tags(tag_id, client_id, name)
tag_assignments(tag_id, target_type, target_id)

6) Task & Follow-Up Tracking

Allow teams to turn insights into actions:

Users can:

create tasks from stories

assign to teammate

mark resolved

Store:

tasks(
  id,
  created_by,
  assigned_to,
  related_target_type,
  related_target_id,
  status,
  due_date
)

7) Watchlists (Persistent Monitoring)

Users can “follow” entities or topics.

Example:
Follow: “Ministry of Finance”

System highlights all future related content.

Store:

watchlists(user_id, entity_or_topic)

8) Internal Alerts Between Team Members

Allow sending internal alerts:

“Check this — may affect our policy briefing.”

Store:

internal_alerts(sender_id, receiver_id, target_id, message, timestamp)

9) Version History (Trust & Accountability)

Track changes to shared reports and annotations.

Store:

change_history(user_id, entity, entity_id, change_type, timestamp)

10) Activity Feed (Collective Awareness)

Create a team activity stream:

Shows:

comments added

reports created

tasks resolved

alerts triggered

Endpoint:

GET /activity-feed
