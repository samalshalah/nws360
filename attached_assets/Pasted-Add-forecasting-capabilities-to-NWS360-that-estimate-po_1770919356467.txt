Add forecasting capabilities to NWS360 that estimate possible future developments using historical patterns, trend velocity, and signal changes. The goal is probabilistic foresight, not certainty.

1) Topic Trajectory Prediction

Estimate where a topic is heading:

For each topic calculate:

momentum (rate of growth)

acceleration (change in growth rate)

media amplification level

actor expansion

Generate:

topic_forecasts(
  topic,
  next_24h_probability,
  next_7d_probability,
  predicted_stage (emerging | escalating | peaking | declining),
  confidence_score
)


Display as:
“Escalation likely within 48 hours (72% confidence)”

2) Early Warning Signals

Detect precursors before major events:

Examples:

sudden appearance of new actors

tone shift before volume spike

clustering across regions

Store:

early_signals(
  signal_type,
  related_topic,
  strength,
  explanation,
  detected_at
)

3) Scenario Modeling (What If Analysis)

Allow users to simulate changes.

User selects:

topic or entity

hypothetical event (policy decision, incident, announcement)

System estimates:

coverage increase likelihood

sentiment impact

related topics activation

Endpoint:

POST /forecast/simulate

4) Risk Scoring

Provide operational risk indicators.

For tracked entities/topics:

risk_scores(
  subject,
  operational_risk,
  reputational_risk,
  escalation_risk,
  confidence
)


This is not prediction — it is probability weighting.

5) Influence Mapping

Identify which sources amplify stories.

Calculate:

which outlets trigger cascades

delay between publication waves

origin vs amplification nodes

Store:

influence_graph(source_a, source_b, influence_strength)

6) Attention Lifespan Estimation

Estimate how long a topic will remain relevant:

attention_decay(topic, estimated_days_remaining)


Display:
“Media attention likely to fade within 3–5 days”

7) Alert Priority Intelligence

Improve alert importance ranking automatically:

High priority if:

accelerating coverage

multi-region spread

rising sentiment volatility

Store:

alert_priority_score(alert_id, score)

8) Forecast Explanation (Transparency Layer)

Every prediction must include reasoning:

Example output:
“Probability increased due to rapid actor expansion and cross-regional pickup.”

Store explanation text with prediction.

9) Forecast Evaluation (Learning Accuracy)

Track prediction outcomes:

forecast_results(
  forecast_id,
  outcome,
  accuracy_score
)


System learns which signals are reliable.

10) Future Briefing

Add a forward-looking report:

Daily section:
What to watch next

Includes:

possible escalations

emerging actors

fading topics
